Events To Register In


PROJECT EXHIBITION!

If you have any cool project to showcase or demonstrate to others, you can present your project in VIDHARA 2K17.

PAPER PRESENTATION!

You can present your paper on cool areas such as Professional Ethics & responsibilities, Image Processing, Computer Networks, Big Data etc.

POSTER PRESENTATION/ADVERTISEMENT

Posters can be made on Engineering solution towards Environment & sustainability.

REGISTRATION

Registration fee

  • Participation for single event is RS 100
  • Participation for two events is RS 150
  • Participation for more than two events is RS 250

Rules & Regulation

  • Maximum number of students per team for any event is 4.
  • Judges decision is final.

Event Wise Rules


  1. PROJECT EXHIBITION: Students must make their own arrangements to exhibit their project.(like laptops, routers, hardware kits etc.
  2. PAPER PRESENTATION: Format of paper must be IEEE format which will not exceed 5 pages. Presentation must not exceed 8 minutes (6+2) and 20 slides. Paper should be mailed to vidara2017rymec@gmail.com
  3. POSTER PRESENTATION: Poster -Students must present their work on a drawing sheet, use ¾ of the sheet for visuals and ¼ of the sheet for text. Advertisement – students should enact and represent advertisement through video for judgment. Time duration Maximum 5 minutes.
  4. QUIZ : There will be 3 rounds, first will be written, another two rounds are buzzer rounds.
  5. CULTURAL ACTIVITIES - Time Duration maximum 5 minutes.
